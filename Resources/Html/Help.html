Browse

Allows you to browse questions and receive immediate feedback on answers. Questions answered in the Browse mode are not scored.

Quiz

Create custom scored and timed quizzes by selecting question type(s) and subject(s).

Scores

View overall performance on all questions, as well as scores by individual quiz and subject.

Select Questions/Create Quiz

After selecting Browse or Create Quiz from the main menu, select the type(s), subject(s) and # of questions you would like to browse or include in your quiz. In the Quiz mode you can enter a name for your quiz. Turn the Timed Quiz button 'ON' to take a timed quiz

Browse Mode

Tap the best answer choice to your question, and press 'Check Answer' to receive feedback. Tap 'View Rationale' to see the rationale for the answer. Swipe left or right to move between questions.

Quiz Mode

Tap the best answer choice to your question, and swipe to move to the next question. Once you have answered all of the questions, tap Review to see a list of all of the questions in the quiz. From the review screen, you can either return to specific questions, or press Submit to see your score. After viewing the score, tap Review Questions to see a list of all of the questions and the answers. Select individual questions to see question and view the rationale.

Timer

If you turned the Timed Quiz button 'ON' while creating your quiz, a timer will appear at the top right of the screen. 1 minute is allocated for each question on your quiz. The timer pauses automatically if you exit the application or switch to another application. Your quiz is submitted and scored once the timer runs out.

Flag

Tap to bookmark a question.

Note

Tap to add a note to a question.
